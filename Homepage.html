Our Services
Chapter 7 Bankruptcy
Our Chapter 7 bankruptcy service offers a way to clear certain debts by liquidating nonexempt assets to pay creditors. Eligible individuals and businesses can discharge qualifying debts while keeping exempt property. There's no repayment plan involved, but some debts and liens remain. Let us help you start fresh with a simple and effective solution tailored to your needs.
Chapter 13 Bankruptcy
Our Chapter 13 bankruptcy service helps individuals with regular income create a 3-5 year repayment plan to manage debts, stop foreclosure, and reschedule certain payments. You'll make payments through a trustee, avoiding direct contact with creditors. It's a great way to protect your assets and simplify your financial life.
Chapter 15 Bankruptcy
Our Chapter 15 bankruptcy service handles cross-border insolvency cases, coordinating U.S. and international law to protect assets and streamline debt management. It allows foreign representatives access to U.S. courts and promotes cooperation between global courts, ensuring efficient solutions for businesses and individuals with international financial ties.
